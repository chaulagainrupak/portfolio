# Rebuilt My Portfolio Site

Over the past few days, I’ve been diving deep into revamping my portfolio site, and I’m excited to finally share it with you! You can check it out [here](https://chaulagainrupak.com.np).

One of the biggest changes? I’ve added a blogging section. This will allow me to share updates, insights, and resources more directly. Kind of like a little corner for all the stuff I’m working on.

Beyond just the portfolio, the site will also act as a hub for some of my other projects. For example:

- **Paperguides** – a resource for students and educators that I’ve been building over time: [paperguides.org](https://paperguides.org)
    - **Past Papers** – if you’re looking for previous exam papers, you can browse them [here](https://paperguides.org/pastpapers).
    - **Questions & Answers Repository** – we’re constantly adding new questions and answers to help learners: [Check it out](https://paperguides.org/questions)

I’d love it if you could take a look at the Q&A repository specifically. It’s a work in progress, and your feedback would be incredibly helpful as we continue to expand it.

This rebuild isn’t just about aesthetics—it’s about functionality, growth, and creating a space where everything I work on can be connected and easily accessible. From the portfolio to Paperguides, everything ties together, and I hope it serves as a useful resource for anyone visiting.

I’m really proud of how it’s shaping up, and I’m excited to continue refining it. If you’ve got a moment, give it a spin, explore the past papers, and let me know what you think about the Q&A section!
